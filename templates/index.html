majestic mountains and looming castles along bucolic rural landscapes

Slovakia is replete with gothic churches, medieval towns, macabre torture inventions, as well as majestic mountains and looming castles along bucolic rural landscapes. Fittingly, legend after legend arose in such surroundings, many of which were based on enigmatic historical figures. All that beauty serve as open invitations to the stretches of imagination. But then again, the best legends rely on a least some smidgeon of truth.During the long cold winter evenings, the villagers grouped together and told stories with magical fiction. These stories have been enriched with elements of reality. Real people appear in them and the places where the stories take place are also real.

Ideas in the people's social environment to be ideas that reflect people's faith in the existence of supernatural forces and beings and in their influence on man. The notions of superstition beings in the spiritual world of our ancestors were diverse. Belief in these beings was perceived as a fact confirmed by the experience of individuals with these beings. Folk faith regarded contact with a supernatural being or phenomenon as an authentic and real experience, and so oral stories or narratives in which our ancestors described experiences with supernatural beings, phenomena, or phenomena spread by word of mouth. Encounters with these beings took place under certain magical circumstances, in mysterious, unusual places or in the forest. .

Castles, chateaux and manors in Slovakia form an important part of immovable cultural monuments. Today, many of these buildings serve the development of culture such as museums, galleries and are made available for tourism. In addition to cultural, educational and aesthetic functions they also represent significant material values. Therefore, it is necessary to protect ,record and document those historical objects.
